BusMall
 

Welcome! This is a web app where you can browse images similar to what you might find in the SkyMall magazine—you know, the one that's tucked into the back-of-seat pocket on most airline flights. In this app, we'll take those products (specifically the office deocration section) and "vote" for your favorite product from each collection of three products shown while you endure your forever-long bus ride around Portland.

You did it! You've voted for 16 products. If you'd like, you can either see the results for those 16 votes, or you can vote for 8 more products and then view your results when you're all done.

So, what'll it be?

All products and images are created by Grovemade.
